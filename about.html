Native Hawaiian and deeply ridiculous, Colton Kekoa Neves once believed his toy chest could fly him to new worlds and has chased that high ever since. He is a graduate of the Clarion Writer's Workshop, Viable Paradise, Aspen Words, and Tin House. He barely scrapes through the winters in Cambridge, MA with his husband and floofy cat, Amelia.

There's a novel in the works and several other stories coming out soon, you know, if they get published.
